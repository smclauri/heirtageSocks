Walk with Them

A Sock Company For Kings & Queens

Heirtage was created to make a statement! These socks are more than just your average sock. Heirtage was created to ensure that minority groups are represented in the revolutionary sock culture that has been taking storm. Each sock is a representation of the strength that our people have showed throughout history. Our socks showcases the trailblazers and those who have made a lasting impact in the world.

A huge part of Heirtage is understanding our culture and being proud of how we look and feel. Our goal is to uplift everyone in the community one sock at a time.
